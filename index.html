Born to Organise

These days a PA is so much more than someone who just types up your letters or arranges meetings. The modern PA is an unflappable multi tasker, a juggler of many balls and the wearer of several hats!

Your Covered

From project managing a refurbishment to organising a last minute travel itinerary, a polished PA is always thinking one step ahead. Researching, negotiating, managing budgets and of course flawless organisational skills are second nature.

Proof in the pudding

With this in mind I set up Ribbon PA to offer my skills to businesses, entrepreneurs and private individuals on a freelance basis.
