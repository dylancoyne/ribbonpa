How can Ribbon help?

Lighten your workload

Whether you’re running your own business, need helping managing a project or just want to tick off some of the tasks on your ever growing personal admin list, there are times when everyone needs an extra pair of hands. Ribbon is here to offer versatile and efficient freelance personal assistance whenever you need it.

Proactive, professional support

From project managing a refurbishment to organising a last minute travel itinerary, a polished PA is always thinking one step ahead. Researching, negotiating, managing budgets and of course flawless organisational skills are second nature at Ribbon.

A PA when you need one

Ribbon offers the flexibility of confidential, high level PA support without the commitment of a full time member of staff. In your office, home or virtually, tasks can be project based, weekly or just ad hoc. Some clients prefer an initial face to face meeting and some just hand over their to do list and leave the rest to Ribbon.

The Ribbon Story

I was born to organise! I have always been passionate about bringing order to the chaos of life and with a brief stint working in TV production, I moved in to the advertising industry. Managing teams of art directors, copywriters and designers whilst working to tight deadlines is no mean feat but my 10 years in the industry taught me great deal of resilience and the ability to always think outside of the box.

I have spent the last 4 years working with a range of clients, providing responsive personal assistance to high net worth and high profile individuals, entrepreneurs and business owners. If it needs trouble - shooting, managing or organizing, I’m there!

Over the years I have built up a book of contacts from IT specialists and web designers to connections at several luxury brands. I am part of a network of high end PA’s who share knowledge on trusted suppliers and if I can’t do something then I usually know someone who can.

I am registered with the Information Commissioner’s Office under the Data Protection Act and I am happy to sign an NDA provided by a client or supply my own. I have full Professional Indemnity and Public Liability insurance.
