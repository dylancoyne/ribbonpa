The Ribbon Story

I was born to organise and I have always been passionate about bringing order to the chaos of life. With a brief stint in TV production, I went on to work at several top 10 London advertising agencies. As a senior project manager I thrived on tight deadlines, while overseeing budgets and managing teams of art directors, copy writers and designers. I learnt a great deal of resilience, the ability to think outside of the box and crucially the solid skill set needed to become a successful and accomplished PA. After a few years working for a prominent business and lifestyle management company I decided to set up Ribbon, offering PA support to high net worth clients on a freelance basis.

I have been providing responsive personal assistance to high net worth and high profile individuals, entrepreneurs and business owners since 2012. If it needs managing, organising or trouble shooting I’m there!

Over the years I have built up a book of contacts from IT specialists and web designers to VIP “fixers”. I am part of a network of high end PA’s who share knowledge on trusted suppliers and if I can’t help then I always know someone who can.

I am registered with the Information Commissioner’s Office under the Data Protection Act and I am happy to sign an NDA provided by a client or supply my own. I have full Professional Indemnity and Public Liability insurance.
